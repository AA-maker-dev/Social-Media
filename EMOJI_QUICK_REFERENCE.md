# 🎯 Emoji Reactions - Quick Reference Card

## Available Reactions

| Emoji | Name | Use When |
|-------|------|----------|
| 👍 | **Like** | You agree or approve |
| ❤️ | **Love** | You really love it |
| 😂 | **Haha** | It made you laugh |
| 😄 | **Smile** | It made you happy |
| 😮 | **Wow** | You're impressed |
| 😍 | **Love It** | You absolutely adore it |
| 🎉 | **Celebrate** | Congratulations or exciting news |
| 🔥 | **Fire** | It's hot, trending, or awesome |

---

## How to React

### Desktop
1. **Hover** over the "Like" button
2. **Click** your favorite emoji

### Mobile
1. **Long-press** the "Like" button (0.5s)
2. **Tap** your favorite emoji

### Quick Like
- Just **click** the Like button for 👍

---

## Features

✅ **One reaction per post** - Choosing new removes old  
✅ **Toggle off** - Click same emoji to remove  
✅ **See breakdown** - Hover over reaction count  
✅ **Smooth animations** - Beautiful effects throughout  
✅ **All positive** - Only happy, supportive reactions

---

## Tips

💡 **Change your mind?** Select a different emoji  
💡 **See what others chose?** Hover over the reaction badges  
💡 **Remove reaction?** Click your current emoji again  
💡 **Mobile user?** Hold button for half a second
