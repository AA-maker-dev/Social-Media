# Cover Image Feature - User Guide

## Overview
Your profile now supports custom cover images! You can upload beautiful images to personalize your profile header.

## Quick Start

### Adding a Cover Image

1. **Navigate to Profile**
   - Go to your profile page (click "Profile" in the navigation menu)

2. **Find the Cover Area**
   - Look at the top of your profile, the large colored area (purple gradient by default)

3. **Hover to Reveal Button**
   - Move your mouse over the cover area
   - A camera icon will appear in the top-right corner

4. **Click to Upload**
   - Click the camera icon
   - A file dialog will open

5. **Select Your Image**
   - Choose an image from your device
   - Supported formats: PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP, etc.
   - Maximum size: 10MB

6. **Done!**
   - Your cover image appears immediately
   - A success message confirms the upload
   - The image is automatically saved

### Removing a Cover Image

1. **Click More Options**
   - Find the "..." (three dots) button on your profile
   - It's located in the profile action buttons

2. **Confirm Removal**
   - A dialog asks you to confirm
   - Click "OK" to remove the cover

3. **Revert to Default**
   - The beautiful purple gradient returns
   - You can upload a new image anytime

## Tips for Best Results

### Image Dimensions
- **Recommended**: 1500px × 500px or wider
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 works best for modern displays
- **Minimum Width**: 600px for mobile compatibility

### Image Quality
- **File Format**: Use PNG or JPG for best compatibility
- **Compression**: Optimize images to stay under 10MB
- **Resolution**: 1440x480px minimum for clarity

### Design Tips
- Choose images that complement your profile
- Avoid busy textures that hide text
- Consider images with lighter areas if you have dark text
- Scenic photos, abstract designs, or gradients work well

### Performance
- Smaller images load faster
- Consider a balance between quality and file size
- Images are stored locally on your device

## Technical Details

### Storage
- Your cover image is saved in your browser's local storage
- It persists when you close and reopen your browser
- Clearing browser data may delete your cover image
- No cloud upload - data stays on your device

### Privacy
- Your cover image is only visible to people who view your profile
- Images are stored locally, not on a server
- You have full control over your image

### Compatibility
- Works on all modern web browsers
- Supports mobile and desktop
- Responsive design adapts to screen size

## Troubleshooting

### "Please select a valid image file"
- Make sure you selected an actual image (PNG, JPG, GIF, etc.)
- File names should have an image extension

### "Image size should be less than 10MB"
- Your image file is too large
- Try compressing it or choosing a smaller image
- Use image compression tools online if needed

### Cover image doesn't appear after refresh
- Check if JavaScript is enabled in your browser
- Try clearing browser cache and reload
- Try uploading the image again

### Button doesn't appear on hover
- Make sure your browser supports hover effects
- On mobile, the button may appear on tap
- Try moving your mouse over the cover area
